PART 1 — MULTIMETER MASTER GUIDE
"Test Before Touch"
A multimeter is your MOST important troubleshooting tool.
It measures:
• Voltage
• Continuity
• Resistance
• Current (amps)
• Sometimes capacitance/frequency
If you misuse it:
• You can blow the meter
• Arc flash yourself
• Destroy equipment
So learn this correctly EARLY.

3. GOLDEN SAFETY RULES
NEVER:
• Measure voltage on amp setting
• Measure resistance on live circuits
• Move leads while touching live power
• Hold metal probe tips
• Trust power is off without testing
4. THE MOST IMPORTANT TESTING RULE
Live → Dead → Live Method
Before checking ANY dead circuit:
Step 1
Test meter on known LIVE source.
Example:
• known working outlet
Test circuit you're working on.
Re-test known LIVE source again.
Step 2 Step 3 This proves:
• meter works
• leads work
• battery isn't dead
Professionals do this EVERY day.

Neutral to Ground

"What if hot-to-neutral reads 0 but hot-to-ground reads 120?"
Likely: Open neutral
Very common troubleshooting issue.
